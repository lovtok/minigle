Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and processes

How long does a typical web design project take?

Most web design projects take 3-6 weeks from start to launch, depending on complexity. Simple websites can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features may take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after the website is built?

Yes! I offer several maintenance plans to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. Maintenance includes security updates, backups, performance monitoring, and content updates if needed. All service packages include at least one month of free maintenance.

Can you help with domain registration and email setup?

Absolutely! I can handle domain registration, SSL certificate setup, and professional email configuration. For complete web solution packages, these are included. For design-only projects, I can assist with these setups for an additional fee.

What happens if I need changes after the website is launched?

I offer flexible support options for post-launch changes. You can choose from hourly rates, retainer packages, or specific maintenance plans. All my websites are built with user-friendly content management systems so you can make basic updates yourself if you prefer.

Do you offer e-commerce solutions for online stores?

Yes, I specialize in e-commerce solutions using platforms like WooCommerce and Shopify. My e-commerce packages include product management, secure payment gateway integration, inventory tracking, and shipping setup. I can create anything from a simple online store to a complex multi-vendor marketplace.

What's included in your hosting plans?

All hosting plans include SSD storage, unlimited bandwidth, free SSL certificates, regular backups, and 24/7 support. Higher-tier plans add priority support, CDN integration, more storage, and the ability to host multiple websites. All hosting comes with a 99.9% uptime guarantee.
