About Alfred Godwin

My journey from a passionate beginner to a web design & hosting specialist with 8+ years of experience

Hello, I'm Alfred Godwin

I'm a dedicated web designer and hosting specialist with over 8 years of experience creating digital solutions that empower businesses to succeed online. My journey in web development began with a simple curiosity about how websites work, which evolved into a passion for creating beautiful, functional digital experiences.

What sets me apart is my unique combination of design sensibility and technical hosting expertise. I don't just create websites – I build complete digital ecosystems that are visually stunning, technically robust, and reliably hosted.

"I believe that a website is more than just an online presence – it's the digital heartbeat of your business. My mission is to create web solutions that not only look great but perform flawlessly, providing your visitors with exceptional experiences while supporting your business growth."

My Professional Journey

The Beginning

Started my journey in web design by creating websites for local small businesses while studying computer science. Built my first e-commerce site for a friend's handmade jewelry business.

2015

Formal Education & First Agency Role

Completed my degree and joined a digital agency as a junior web developer. Learned the intricacies of responsive design, SEO, and user experience principles while working on diverse client projects.

2017

Specialization in Hosting

Recognized the critical importance of reliable hosting and began specializing in server management and optimization. Started offering hosting services alongside my web design work.

2019

Launching Minigle

Founded Minigle to offer comprehensive web design and hosting solutions. Developed my signature approach that combines aesthetic design with technical excellence and reliable hosting infrastructure.

2021

Present Day & Future Vision

Now serving clients worldwide with a focus on creating sustainable digital solutions. Continuously learning and adapting to new technologies while maintaining my commitment to quality and client satisfaction.

2024
