Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about starting a project with me

How long does a typical web design project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features or e-commerce functionality may take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer hosting services as part of your packages?

Yes! I offer comprehensive hosting solutions with all my web design packages. This includes secure hosting, regular backups, SSL certificates, and technical support. You can also choose to host elsewhere if you prefer.

What's included in the project cost?

My project costs include design, development, testing, basic SEO setup, responsive optimization, and one month of post-launch support. Hosting is billed separately on a monthly or annual basis. I provide a detailed breakdown in your project proposal.

Will I be able to update my website myself?

Absolutely! I build all websites with user-friendly content management systems (like WordPress) that allow you to easily update text, images, and content. I also provide training and documentation to help you manage your site.

What's your payment process?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment details will be clearly outlined in our project agreement.
